YouTube was created by three former PayPal employees:
Chad Hurley, Steve Chen, and Jawed Karim. The website was launched on February 14, 2005.
The idea for YouTube reportedly came about because of difficulties the founders experienced when trying to share videos that were taken at a dinner party.
The platform quickly gained popularity, and in November 2006, it was acquired by Google for $1.65 billion in stock.

Youtube Kids

YouTube Kids, launched in 2015, provides a curated selection of child-friendly content, including educational videos,
cartoons, and music, aimed at offering a safer online experience for children. The app offers parental controls, allowing parents to set screen time limits, restrict content,
and disable search functionality. Content is filtered using a combination of automated systems and human reviewers, with partnerships with various content creators
and organizations to ensure quality and diversity. Available globally on mobile devices and select smart TVs, YouTube Kids also offers a subscription-based ad-free option.
With a focus on continuous improvement and feedback, it features educational content from renowned channels like PBS Kids and National Geographic Kids,
making it a popular choice for parents seeking a safe and enriching digital environment for their children.

Youtube music

YouTube Music is a music streaming service launched by YouTube in 2018, providing users with access to a vast library of songs, music videos, and playlists.
It offers personalized recommendations based on user preferences, listening history, and trending tracks. YouTube Music allows users to discover new music, create playlists,
and download songs for offline listening. It also features music videos, live performances, and artist interviews. The service is available as a standalone app and is included as
part of YouTube Premium subscription, which offers an ad-free experience and additional features. With its extensive music catalog and integration with the YouTube platform,
YouTube Music has become a popular choice for music lovers worldwide.
